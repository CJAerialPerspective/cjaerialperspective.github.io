About

I come from a hands-on property background—years working in the building trades then moving into inventory and property documentation.

Today, I work closely with estate agents, landlords, and property managers across Bournemouth and Poole, providing structured, visual reporting that supports real decisions—not just images.

The difference is context. I don't just capture a roof—I understand what I'm seeing. From early signs of failure to visible installation issues, the focus is always on delivering clear, usable insight.

This is a natural extension of the work I already do—now adding an aerial perspective where it genuinely adds value.
